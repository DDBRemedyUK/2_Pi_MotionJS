From clinical trials to clinical practice:

real world data for HARVONI

  • “I’m cured! I couldn’t believe it when I got the results. Now the future looks bright for me and
    the children.”
  • “My treatment was quite simple – just one pill each morning while the children had their breakfast.”
  • “The sooner the treatment was done, the sooner we could get on with our lives – it only took
    2 months.”
  • Up to 100% cure in real-world HCV GT1 treatment-naïve patients without cirrhosis treated with 8-week HARVONI (n=558)1–4,a,b
  • HARVONI offers a single-tablet regimen for the majority of HCV GT1 patients.5,c 96% treatment adherence was observed in real-world patients treated with 8-week HARVONI3,d
  • 95–100% completion rates were seen in real-world patients treated with
    8-week HARVONI1–4
  • “There’s a lot of stigma around having HCV, especially being coinfected. But now I’m cured, I don’t have to worry about it.”
  • “I didn’t even have to change my HIV medication – that was such
    a relief.”
  • “My job is important and I can’t afford to take time off. With this treatment, I didn’t have to.”
  • Up to 100% cure in real-world HIV/HCV-coinfected patients treated with HARVONI for 8 weeks1,a,b
  • HARVONI can be coadministered with all classes of HIV
    antiviral agents2,c
  • HARVONI is well-tolerated; up to 100% of real-world patients completed 8-week therapy
    with HARVONI1,3,4,d
  • “My friend who was treated needed ribavirin because of their genotype. My doctor said it wouldn’t
    be necessary.”
  • “Of course I would have taken the treatment for as long as needed, but 2 months instead of 3 made things a lot easier.”
  • “I recently got my blood pressure under control, and I was a bit worried what it would mean for my treatment, but my doctor told me it shouldn’t be a problem.”
  • “I also take some pills for an ulcer. My doctor said it was important to make sure I took them at the same time or after my HCV tablet. I didn’t find it difficult!”
  • 8-week HARVONI achieved high cure rates in real-world patients, regardless of GT1 subtype1–4,a,b
  • In clinical practice, 8-week HARVONI was as effective as 12-week treatment in eligible patients1,a
  • Up to 100% cure rates were seen in real-world patients with a range
    of comorbidities3
  • Coadministration of HARVONI with acid-reducing agents is not contraindicated.5 In order to ensure maximum absorption of the active components of HARVONI, it is important to adhere to the recommended dosage timings.5
  • “They told me my liver was quite bad, but I got cured anyway.”
  • “I wouldn’t have done the
    treatment if I’d needed to stop
    my methadone.”
  • “I can forget things, but I took my tablets every day like the doctor told me to.”
  • 8-week treatment with HARVONI resulted in consistently high cure rates in patients without cirrhosis, regardless of fibrosis score1,a,b
  • No dose adjustment is required
    when coadministering HARVONI
    with narcotics2,c
  • 96% treatment adherence was observed in real-world patients treated with 8-week HARVONI3,d
  1. Cure rates of 95–100% were observed in four real-world studies of treatment-naïve HCV GT1 patients without cirrhosis, treated with 8-week HARVONI.1–4
  2. Patients in real world studies reported comorbidities including depression, arterial hypertension, aortic sclerosis, and anaemia.3
